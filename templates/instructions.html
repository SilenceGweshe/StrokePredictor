Welcome to the Stroke Risk Predictor

This tool helps you assess the likelihood of Ischemic stroke based on key health indicators.

How to Use:

  • Ensure you're connected to the internet.
  • Click the button below to go to the prediction form.
  • Fill in all required details (like age, BMI, glucose level, etc.).
  • Click "Predict" to get your stroke risk assessment.

Troubleshooting

  • If the app doesn't load, refresh your browser or try a different one.
  • If prediction fails, ensure all fields are filled correctly.
